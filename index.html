About Kigumo TVC

  • Founded: 2023, serving Murang'a and beyond
  • Accreditation: Registered by TVETA, Ministry of Education
  • Vision: To be a center of excellence in technical and vocational training
  • Core Values: Integrity, Innovation, Inclusivity, Environmental Stewardship
  • Facilities: Modern workshops, ICT labs, Jitume Labs, outside - hostels, High Speed Internet(WI-FI) and sports grounds
  • Community: Over 2,000+ students and 60+ staff
