VISION & MISSION

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in technical and vocational training, producing competent and innovative graduates for national and global development.

Our Mission

To provide quality, industry‑aligned training, research, and community service through modern facilities, skilled staff, and a supportive learning environment.

Core Values

  • Integrity and accountability
  • Innovation and creativity
  • Inclusivity and respect for diversity
  • Professionalism and excellence
  • Environmental stewardship
  • Community engagement and service

Our Core Functions & Mandate

  • Offer market‑driven artisan, certificate, and diploma programmes.
  • Equip learners with practical skills for employment and entrepreneurship.
  • Promote research, innovation, and technology transfer.
  • Provide career guidance, mentorship, and student support services.
  • Foster industry linkages, partnerships, and apprenticeship opportunities.
  • Deliver community outreach and capacity‑building initiatives.
