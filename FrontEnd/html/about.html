About Kigumo TVC

Building skills for employment, entrepreneurship, and community impact.

Our History

Kigumo Technical and Vocational College (KTVC) was established to expand access to quality technical training for Murang'a County and beyond. From a modest start, the college has grown into a vibrant institution offering artisan, certificate, and diploma programmes aligned to industry needs.

Over the years, we have invested in modern workshops, ICT labs, and a supportive learning environment that promotes innovation, entrepreneurship, and community service.

Who We Are

KTVC is a public TVET institution committed to producing competent graduates with practical skills for employment and self‑reliance.

  • Industry‑aligned training
  • Competency‑based approach
  • Strong partnerships and linkages
  • Student‑centred support services

Admissions & How to Apply

Application Steps

1. Choose your preferred programme.
2. Collect or download the application form.
3. Attach required documents (ID/Birth Certificate, academic certificates, and passport photo).
4. Submit to the admissions office or via the online portal.
5. Await admission feedback and reporting instructions.
